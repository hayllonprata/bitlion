      Frequently asked questions

      What is a cryptocurrency exchange? Cryptocurrency exchanges are digital marketplaces that enable users to buy and sell cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Tether. The Bitcat exchange is the largest crypto exchange by trade volume.
      What products does Bitcat provide? Bitcat is the world's leading cryptocurrency exchange, catering to 235 million registered users in over 180 countries. With low fees and over 350 cryptocurrencies to trade, Bitcat is the preferred exchange to trade Bitcoin, Altcoins, and other virtual assets.
      How to buy Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies on Bitcat? There are several ways to buy cryptocurrencies on Bitcat. You can use a credit/debit card, cash balance, or Apple Pay/Google Pay to purchase crypto on Bitcat. Before getting started, please make sure you’ve completed Identity Verification for your Bitcat account.
      How to track cryptocurrency prices? The easiest way to track the latest cryptocurrency prices, trading volumes, trending altcoins, and market cap is the Bitcat Cryptocurrency Directory. Click on the coins to know historical coin prices, 24-hour trading volume, and the price of cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, BNB and others in real-time.
      How to trade cryptocurrencies on Bitcat? You can trade hundreds of cryptocurrencies on Bitcat via the Spot, Margin, Futures, and Options markets. To begin trading, users need to register an account, complete identity verification, buy/deposit crypto, and start trading.
      How to earn from crypto on Bitcat? Users can earn rewards on more than 180+ cryptocurrencies by using one of the products offered on Bitcat Earn. Our platform offers dozens of digital assets like Bitcoin, Ethereum, and stablecoins.
